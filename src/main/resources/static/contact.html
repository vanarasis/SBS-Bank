Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions.

You can open an account online in just a few minutes by visiting our account opening page. You'll need to provide some personal information and identification documents. Alternatively, you can visit any of our branches to open an account in person.

If you notice any suspicious activity on your account, please contact our fraud department immediately at 1-800-123-4567 (option 3). We recommend changing your online banking password and reviewing your recent transactions. Our team will guide you through the next steps to secure your account.

To reset your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. You'll need to verify your identity by answering security questions or entering a code sent to your registered email or phone number. After verification, you can create a new password.
